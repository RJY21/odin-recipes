Omelette Rice

A delicious rice dish that is simple yet satisfying

This rice dish is super easy to make and tastes amazing!

  • Eggs
  • Rice
  • Spam
  • Onion
  • Carrot

Steps

  1. Crack four eggs into hot pan and make omelette - place omelette aside
  2. Chop the Spam, onion, and carrots into small pieces and stir fry with the rice
  3. Place omelette onto the stir-fry, top with ketchup and enjoy!
