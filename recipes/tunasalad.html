Tuna Salad

A delicious tuna salad that is simple yet satisfying

This tuna salad recipe is super versatile - enjoy on its own, make it a sandwich, or even eat it with crackers!

Ingredients

  • Tuna
  • Mayo
  • Celery
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Lemon Juice
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Get a medium-sized bowl and add tuna, mayo, celery, onion, lemon juice, and garlic.
  2. Stir to combine and season to taste with salt and pepper
