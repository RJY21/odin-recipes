Protein Shake

A delicious protein shake that is tasty and nutritious

This protein shake is perfect for a quick and healthy meal or snack

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Protein Powder
  • Berry Mix
  • PB2 Powder
  • Banana

Steps

  1. Pour milk into blender
  2. Put banana, two scoops of protein powder, half scoop of PB2 powder, berry mix, and banana into the blender
  3. Blend and enjoy
